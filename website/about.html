About hack3r.gg

We believe security knowledge should be open, accessible, and actionable. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical defense.

Our Mission

hack3r.gg was founded with a simple belief: the security community grows stronger when knowledge is shared openly. We publish in-depth research, practical tutorials, and timely analysis to help defenders stay ahead of evolving threats.

We're not just another security blog. We're a platform built by practitioners, for practitioners. Every piece of content is reviewed for technical accuracy and written with real-world applicability in mind.

What We Stand For

Open Knowledge

Security through obscurity doesn't work. We publish research openly so defenders everywhere can benefit from our findings and strengthen their posture.

Timely Analysis

When new vulnerabilities emerge, we move fast. Our team provides rapid, accurate analysis so you can assess risk and respond effectively.

Practical Focus

Theory matters, but implementation matters more. Every article includes actionable steps, working code, and real-world guidance you can apply immediately.

Community First

We actively support the security community through open-source contributions, CTF sponsorships, and mentorship programs for newcomers to the field.

Our Journey

2022

hack3r.gg launches

Started as a personal blog by Alex Hayes, sharing vulnerability research and CTF writeups with the community.

2023

Team expansion

Grew to a team of four with dedicated researchers. Launched our first open-source security tool, reaching 2K GitHub stars.

2024

Community programs

Launched the hack3r.gg CTF series and mentorship program. Published our 100th research article. Hit 25K monthly readers.

2025

Research recognition

Discovered 20+ CVEs across major platforms. Research cited by CISA and NIST. Crossed 50K monthly readers.

2026

What's next

Expanding into interactive labs, video content, and deeper community tooling. The best is ahead.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute an article?

We welcome contributions from the security community. Send us a pitch through the contact form above with "Content Contribution" as the subject. Include a brief outline of your topic, your background, and any relevant writing samples.

How do you handle vulnerability reports?

We follow a coordinated disclosure process. If you've discovered a vulnerability and need help with disclosure, email security@hack3r.gg with details. We'll work with you and the affected vendor to ensure responsible handling.

Do you offer sponsorships for CTF teams?

Yes, we sponsor select CTF teams and security competitions. Reach out with details about your team, recent placements, and what support you're looking for.

Can I use your research in my work?

Our published research is available under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0. You're free to reference and share it with proper attribution. For commercial use beyond standard citation, please contact us.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

Our team regularly presents at security conferences and corporate events. Select "Speaking / Events" in the contact form and include event details, dates, and topic areas of interest.

What's your response time?

We aim to respond to general inquiries within 2 business days. Security reports are triaged within 24 hours. For urgent security matters, please mark your email accordingly.
